Harvest Hero Origins

An arcade wave defense game with local co-op, unlockable playable characters and skins.

A young hero picks up a hammer to defend his home from the onslaught of the Grooble army. Defend the village of Fronvale and become the Harvest Hero!

About Gemdrop Games

Clay Paris

Clay founded Gemdrop Games in 2021 when he began to pursue his lifelong dream of making games. He's played games since he was a child and started programming and learning game design around the end of High School. Currently a solo developer, he commissions artwork and audio from other indie studios.
